Entity Control Board Chair

ECB Chair | Term 26.27

Role Overview

The Entity Control Board Chair is responsible for overseeing governance, compliance, and organizational standards across all local entities of AIESEC in Sri Lanka. This role ensures that entities operate within established frameworks and maintain the highest standards of accountability.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and manage the Entity Control Board operations nationally
  • Monitor entity compliance with organizational standards and policies
  • Conduct audits and assessments of local committee operations
  • Develop and implement governance improvement strategies
  • Report entity performance and compliance status to MC
  • Support entities in achieving organizational benchmarks

What We're Looking For

  • Strong understanding of AIESEC governance and compliance frameworks
  • Excellent analytical and organizational skills
  • Previous leadership experience in AIESEC (LC or national level preferred)
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple stakeholders
  • Strong communication and reporting capabilities

Dev Team Lead

Development & Technology | Term 26.27

Role Overview

The Dev Team Lead spearheads all technology and digital development initiatives for AIESEC in Sri Lanka. This role drives innovation, builds digital solutions, and leads the development team to create tools and platforms that empower the organization's operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead the national development team and manage project timelines
  • Design and develop digital solutions for organizational needs
  • Maintain and improve existing digital infrastructure
  • Coordinate with other functional areas to identify tech solutions
  • Drive data-driven decision making through analytics tools
  • Train and upskill team members on relevant technologies

What We're Looking For

  • Proficiency in web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, frameworks)
  • Experience with project management and team leadership
  • Knowledge of data analytics and visualization tools
  • Passion for using technology to create social impact
  • Previous AIESEC experience preferred

Entity Finance Board Chair

EFB Chair | Term 26.27

Role Overview

The Entity Finance Board Chair is responsible for overseeing the financial governance, transparency, and sustainability of all local entities of AIESEC in Sri Lanka. This role ensures sound financial practices across the network and supports entities in achieving financial health.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and manage the Entity Finance Board nationally
  • Oversee financial audits and compliance across all local entities
  • Develop financial reporting frameworks and standards
  • Support entities with budgeting, financial planning, and sustainability
  • Collaborate with MC Finance to align national financial strategies
  • Identify and address financial risks across the entity network

What We're Looking For

  • Strong understanding of financial management and accounting principles
  • Experience with financial auditing and compliance
  • Previous leadership experience in AIESEC (LC or national level preferred)
  • Excellent attention to detail and analytical thinking
  • Ability to train and coach others on financial best practices
